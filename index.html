SUNDAY WORKSHOPS

Looking for free dance workshops? RDC hosts workshops every Sunday during the Fall and Spring semesters. Each Sunday night you will get the opportunity to learn 2 pieces of choreography from current RDC members. If this sounds interesting to you, please keep an eye out on our Instagram @redefineddanceco

WRECK THEM PEOPLES DANCE CONVENTION

Every year RDC hosts our annual Wreck Them People's Dance Convention, bringing in notable choreographers from all over Texas to one place for one weekend. All proceeds go to our charity of choice.
